Al Pastor

Al pastor is a taco made with spit-grilled pork.

Carne Asada

Carne asada is a dish of grilled and sliced beef, usually skirt steak, sirloin steak.

Carnitas

Carnitas are made by braising or simmering pork in oil or preferably lard until tender

Barbacoa

Barbacoa is an authentic Mexican dish typically made with beef, goat or lamb.

Chicharron

Chicharon are pork skin cracklings that are made from pork rind and a little bit of fat.

El Pollo
